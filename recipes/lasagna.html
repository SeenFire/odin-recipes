Lasagna Recipe

Description

We will learn how to make a lasagna here!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Dried parsley, Salt and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the sphagetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
